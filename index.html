A little bit about myself

Hi there! I'm Anna, the creative heart behind Gathered by the Door. My journey into this beautiful business began with a simple truth: I am absolutely in love with the holidays and the magic that comes with decorating spaces.

15+ Years of Design & Decorating Experience

For over fifteen years, I've been channeling my creativity and passion for design into transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. What started as a personal love for holiday decorating has blossomed into a calling to bring that same joy and wonder to your home.

I believe that every entrance tells a story, and every seasonal display has the power to create lasting memories. Whether it's the perfect pumpkin arrangement for fall, twinkling lights for the holidays, or a custom celebration setup, I pour my heart into every project because I know how much these moments mean to families.

When I'm not designing magical displays, you'll find me exploring new creative techniques, sourcing the most beautiful seasonal elements, and dreaming up ways to make your next gathering even more special. Thank you for letting me be part of your story!
